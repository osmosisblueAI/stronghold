What We Offer

At Stronghold Deconstruction, we provide a comprehensive range of demolition, cleanup, and haul-off services to meet the needs of residential clients throughout Northeast Ohio. Our team has the expertise, equipment, and experience to handle projects of all sizes safely and efficiently.

Residential Demolition

Our residential demolition services include everything from partial home demolition to selective interior demolition. Whether you're preparing for a new build or a major renovation, our team ensures a safe and efficient demolition process.

Our Residential Services Include:
  • Partial home demolition
  • Shed and small structure removal
  • Interior demolition for renovations
  • Kitchen and bathroom demolition
  • Deck and porch removal
  • Concrete removal

Interior Demolition
